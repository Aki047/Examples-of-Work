Education Thru Entertainment

Grimm History is presented by Sean Grimm, and American Historian, Storyteller, Historical Researcher, and Presenter. He believes in “Education through Entertainment”- the process of bringing history, information, and hard facts to audiences by way of storytelling. This is done by presenting topics in an almost conversational way, thus making the audience feel comfortable and appreciated. Sean’s presentations have a way of making dull material become colorful and alive, and when presented in this manner, people have an easier time engaging and enjoying the material. Whether it’s discussing the relevance of historical events or the impact of public policies, Sean has a way of making the conversation memorable and fun.
